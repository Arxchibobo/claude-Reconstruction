What is Vibe Marketing?

A plug-and-play system designed for creators, marketers, and founders who want to move fast and build momentum.

Core Formula

Research > Strategy > Content > Revenue

Traditional Approach

2+ weeks of manual research, fragmented tools, inconsistent output

Vibe Marketing

~1 hour automated pipeline, integrated tools, systematic output

Two Pillars

1. Marketing Mastery

  • Discover profitable niches with Claude + Google data
  • Auto-generate multi-channel content
  • Prompt chains for strategy & positioning
  • ROI-backed content systems
  • Anti-AI authenticity guidelines

2. AI System Building

  • MCP server setup (Claude + n8n + APIs)
  • Git-powered automation workflows
  • Research-to-publish automation
  • Content ops pipeline (Claude + Apify + Supabase)
  • AI agents that think, not just act
